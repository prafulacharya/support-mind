# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

## Account & Login

### How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, follow these steps:
1. Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password"
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for a password reset link (may take up to 5 minutes)
4. Click the link and enter your new password
5. Log in with your new password

**Important:** Password reset links expire after 24 hours. If your link expires, simply request a new one.

### How do I change my email address?

To change your email address:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Settings > Account
3. Click "Change Email"
4. Enter your new email address
5. You'll receive a confirmation email at both the old and new addresses
6. Confirm the change by clicking the link in the email sent to your new address

### Can I have multiple accounts?

Each person can have only one active account per email address. However, you can have separate accounts for different email addresses if needed.

## Billing & Payments

### What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:
- Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
- Debit cards
- PayPal
- Bank transfers (for annual plans)

All payments are processed securely through our payment partner Stripe.

### What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied with our service, you can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

To request a refund:
1. Contact our support team with your order ID
2. Include a brief explanation of why you'd like a refund
3. We'll process your refund within 5 business days

**Note:** Refunds are only available for initial purchases. Renewals cannot be refunded.

### Can I change my billing cycle?

Yes, you can change your billing cycle anytime:
- Monthly to yearly: Changes take effect on your next renewal date with prorated credit
- Yearly to monthly: Changes take effect immediately with a pro-rata adjustment

Go to Settings > Billing to make changes.

### Do you offer discounts for annual plans?

Yes! Annual plans come with a 20% discount compared to monthly billing. For example:
- Monthly: $29/month = $348/year
- Annual: $279/year (save $69)

We also offer volume discounts for teams. Contact our sales team for a custom quote.

## Orders & Shipping

### How do I track my order?

To track your order:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "Order History"
3. Click on the order you want to track
4. You'll see a tracking number with a link to the carrier's website
5. Click the tracking link to see real-time updates

**Note:** It may take 24 hours after purchase for tracking information to appear.

### What is your shipping timeline?

- **Standard Shipping:** 5-7 business days (Free)
- **Express Shipping:** 2-3 business days ($15)
- **Overnight Shipping:** 1 business day ($25)

Shipping times start from the date of order processing (after payment confirmation).

### Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to over 150 countries! International orders typically take 10-21 business days depending on the destination.

International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on weight and destination.

### What if my order doesn't arrive?

If your order doesn't arrive within the promised timeframe:
1. First check your tracking information
2. If the carrier shows it as delivered but you haven't received it, check with neighbors/mail room
3. Wait 2-3 additional business days (sometimes packages are delayed in transit)
4. If still not received, contact us with your order ID and tracking number

We'll investigate and either reship your order or process a full refund.

## Technical Support

### The app is running slowly. What can I do?

Try these troubleshooting steps:
1. Clear your browser cache and cookies
2. Try a different browser
3. Disable browser extensions (especially ad blockers)
4. Restart your computer
5. Check your internet connection speed

If the problem persists:
- Try accessing from a different device
- Contact us with your account details and a description of what's slow

### I'm getting an error message. How do I fix it?

Error messages usually appear for specific reasons:
- **"Unauthorized"**: Check that you're logged in. Try logging out and logging back in.
- **"Quota exceeded"**: You've hit your usage limit. Upgrade your plan or wait until your quota resets.
- **"Invalid input"**: Check that all required fields are filled out correctly.
- **"Server error"**: This is a temporary issue on our end. Try again in a few minutes.

If the error persists, please contact support with:
- The exact error message
- A screenshot if possible
- When it happens
- Your account email

### How do I export my data?

To export your data:
1. Go to Settings > Data & Privacy
2. Click "Export My Data"
3. Select the data format (CSV, JSON, or PDF)
4. We'll prepare your export within 24 hours
5. You'll receive a download link via email

### Can I integrate with other tools?

We support integrations with:
- Zapier
- Slack
- GitHub
- Google Workspace
- Microsoft Teams
- Salesforce (enterprise plans only)

Go to Settings > Integrations to see all available options and setup instructions.
